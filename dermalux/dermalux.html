Our Light Therapies

Blue Light

Anti-bacterial action that treats spots without irritation. Improves skin clarity and has a calming effect for problem skin.

Red Light

Boosts collagen and elastin production, reduces fine lines and wrinkles, and increases hydration. Evens skin tone and texture while revitalising and restoring radiance. Anti-inflammatory benefits.

Near IR Light

Advanced skin rejuvenation benefits that calm redness and irritation. Reduces age spots and pigmentation, restores skin health, and is ideal for the most sensitive skins.
