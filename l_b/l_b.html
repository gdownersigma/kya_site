A bit about the treatments

Brow Lamination

Our luxury brow lamination treatment, performed exclusively with Buff Brows professional products, is designed to smooth, lift and refine your natural brow hairs for a fuller, sulpted finish. This advanced restructuring service realigns each hair into your desired shape, creating a soft, brushed-up effect or a sleek, polished arch tailored to you. The result is effortlessly elevated brows that appear thicker, more symmetrical, and beautifully styled. This lasts 6 to 8 weeks with proper aftercare . A perfect choice for those seeking a refined yet low-maintenance brow enhancement.

Hybrid Brow Tinting

Our hybrid brow tinting service, using premium Buff Brows formulations, delivers rich, long-lasting colour with a flawless, softly defined finish. This innovative tint stains both the brow hairs and the skin beneath, creating depth and fullness while maintaining a sophisticated, natural appearance. Ideal for enhancing shape, filling sparse areas, and achieving a subtle make-up effect, hybrid tinting offers beautifully defined brows that last 7 to 10 days on the skin and up to 6 weeks on the hairs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your appointment

Lash lift, Lash & Brow tint, Hybrid Brow tint and Brow lamination. All of these treatments require a patch test 48 hours in advance, this to check for any potential allergic reactions.

With the Novalash method of lash extensions, there is no need for a patch test as there is no skin contact at all during the treatment. However, if you have ever reacted to lash treatments in the past, it is important that you let us know at the time of booking, and again during the consultation part of your treatment.

We will normally schedule an appointment of 1–2 hours, primarily to ensure that we cover everything in your consultation, although the application normally takes around an hour.

We would ask that you do not wear any mascara to your appointment, to ensure there is a clean base with which to start.
